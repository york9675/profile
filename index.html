About

When I started developing apps, my goal was simple but sincere: to create things that are genuinely helpful and provide them for free. In a world full of paywalled features and ads, I want to build tools anyone can use at no cost, purely to make life more convenient. My apps have always been ad-free, and some are even open source. I believe that even if they only help a small number of people—making life easier or more efficient—that alone makes it worthwhile.

Anyway, enough rambling. I’m interested in aviation, music (mainly), and tech (secondarily), and I’m definitely an introvert.
